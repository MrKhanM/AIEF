# Purpose

The Product Constitution defines the principles that guide every decision made while designing, developing, and evolving the Artificial Intelligence Engineering Framework (AIEF).

Every feature, architectural decision, workflow, module, and business decision should align with this constitution.

---

# Why AIEF Exists

Artificial Intelligence has become incredibly powerful.

However, people still spend significant time deciding which AI to use, rewriting prompts, managing context, validating responses, and coordinating work across multiple tools.

AIEF exists to eliminate this complexity.

Instead of users managing AI systems, AIEF enables AI systems to work together on behalf of the user.

---

# Our Beliefs

## 1. AI should complete work, not simply answer prompts.

Success is measured by completed outcomes rather than generated responses.

---

## 2. Workflows are more valuable than conversations.

Complex tasks require planning, execution, validation, and iteration.

---

## 3. Every AI has strengths.

No single AI model is best at every task.

AIEF should intelligently leverage the strengths of multiple providers.

---

## 4. Models are replaceable.

The framework should never depend on one AI provider.

AI providers are interchangeable components.

---

## 5. Memory is essential.

Projects should continuously accumulate knowledge.

Users should never need to repeatedly explain the same project.

---

## 6. Quality is a feature.

Every important output should be reviewed, validated, and improved before reaching the user.

---

## 7. Humans remain in control.

AIEF assists decision-making.

It never removes human ownership of important decisions.

---

# Design Principles

Every feature added to AIEF should satisfy these principles.

- Simplicity
- Transparency
- Explainability
- Extensibility
- Modularity
- Scalability
- Reliability
- Security

---

# Decision Framework

Before implementing any feature, ask:

1. Does it help users complete meaningful work?

2. Does it reduce complexity?

3. Does it improve collaboration?

4. Is it reusable?

5. Is it platform independent?

6. Would it remain useful if AI models improve dramatically?

If the answer is "No" to most of these questions, reconsider the feature.

---

# What We Will Not Build

AIEF is not:

- A chatbot wrapper.
- A prompt library.
- A prompt marketplace.
- A replacement for existing AI providers.
- A no-code automation platform.

AIEF is an orchestration platform that coordinates AI capabilities to solve complex work.

---

# Definition of Success

AIEF succeeds when users no longer think about which AI model to use.

Instead, they focus entirely on the work they want to accomplish.
